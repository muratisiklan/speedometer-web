What the App Does

Speedometer Lite is a GPS-based speedometer application that:

  • Displays your current speed based on GPS data from your device
  • Tracks distance traveled during trips
  • Records trip history including routes, distances, and speed data
  • Shows your position on a map
  • Provides optional telemetry displays like altitude and heading
  • Offers customizable themes and display preferences

The app is designed for informational and recreational purposes.

Important Safety Information

Safety first: GPS speed readings can vary due to signal quality, environmental conditions, and device limitations. Do not rely solely on this app for critical speed or navigation decisions.

GPS Accuracy

The speed displayed in the app is calculated from GPS signals received by your device. GPS accuracy can be affected by:

  • Signal obstructions (tunnels, tall buildings, dense tree cover)
  • Atmospheric conditions
  • Your device's GPS hardware quality
  • The time since GPS was last used (initial lock time)

The app displays a GPS signal indicator to help you understand current signal quality, but this is an estimate and not a guarantee of accuracy.

Safe Usage

The app is not a replacement for your vehicle's speedometer or any certified speed measurement device.
