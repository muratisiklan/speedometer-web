Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Simple, focused tools for tracking your speed and journeys.

📍

GPS-Based Speed

Uses your device's GPS to display your current speed with high accuracy. Works while driving, cycling, or any movement.

🗺️

Trip Recording

Record your journeys with distance, duration, average speed, and max speed. View your trips on a map with the route traced.

🎨

12 Beautiful Themes

Choose from Minimal, Futuristic, Automotive, Aviation, Vintage, and more. Each theme includes a matching app icon.

📊

Live Telemetry

See altitude, heading, and optional G-force visualization. Real-time data displayed in a clean, readable format.

🌐

Multi-Language

Available in 11 languages including English, Spanish, German, French, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and more.

⚙️

Customizable Units

Switch between km/h, mph, and knots. Supports both metric and imperial units for distance and altitude.

🚗

HUD Mode

Place your phone on the dashboard and use the mirrored display to project your speed onto the windshield at night.

🔔

Speed Alerts

Set custom speed limits and get haptic or sound alerts when you exceed them. Perfect for staying within speed limits.

🔒

Your data stays with you

Speedometer Lite processes all location data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit your location, trips, or any personal information to external servers.
